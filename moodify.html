Moodify

Project Date May 1, 2023
Team Jaydeep, Ojas,Tejas
Tech-Used Flutter,Dart,ML,Python
Visit jaydeeppatel17/Moodify_Apk.git

"The project is a Flutter APK that utilizes machine learning to detect facial expressions and determine the user's mood in real-time. By analyzing the user's facial expressions using a trained machine learning model, the application identifies the predominant mood, such as happiness, sadness, or anger. Based on the detected mood, the app then plays appropriate music from a predefined library or suggests relevant playlists.

The application provides an interactive and personalized experience, enhancing the user's mood through music selection that aligns with their emotional state.
